What is Everon Life?
Everon Life is a community-driven Arma Reforger RPG open source framework. “Community-driven” is not just a buzzword. Our vision is to develop a framework with and for the community. There are almost no restrictions regarding participation. Through our high quality standards, our love for the community and our mature collaboration guidelines, we guarantee full control and transparency.
Who are we?
The team is made up of experienced Arma and DayZ veterans who have done impressive work over the last decade. We are deeply rooted in the Bohemia Interactive universe. To ensure the success of this endeavor, it requires a passionate community and a dedicated core team. In the near future, we will publish a Core Team page where we introduce ourselves as a team.
Why Open Source?
In the past we have often seen and experienced hate, envy and strife in almost all communities. We want to prevent this from happening again by offering an open and free framework that benefits everyone. We want to direct the energy of the community in a positive direction to create something awesome together.
